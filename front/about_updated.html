About José Aparecido dos Santos, Unipessoal Lda

Based in Lisbon and with over 10 years of experience, José Aparecido dos Santos, Unipessoal Lda, is a company that stands out in the production of high-quality audiovisual content. Founded in 2014, it has built an extensive portfolio with over 600 high-quality filming participations, including films, videos, and television programs, also offering specialized services in makeup and hairstyling.

At the forefront of this brand is a professional whose story inspires perseverance and overcoming challenges, dedicating himself to the art of transforming ideas into visually striking productions. With each project, the company reaffirms its commitment to innovation and excellence, being a reliable and creative partner for discerning clients.

To learn more about the work and get inspired by the creations already made, we invite you to explore the company's social media and discover the universe of possibilities offered by the talent and dedication of the man leading this remarkable journey.
